Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does an interior designer do?

An interior designer designs space and they make sure you get the best experience from the room. An interior designer place all the furniture and electrical equipment so that the people living in the room are in their best possible comfort.

2. How much does interior design services cost?

The cost of interior design services can vary widely depending on the scope of the project, your location, and the level of customization. We offer a free initial consultation to discuss your needs and provide a customized quote based on your budget and preferences.

3. Can you provide references or a portfolio of your previous work?

Yes, we have a portfolio of our past projects that showcase our design expertise. We can also provide references from satisfied clients upon request.

4. What is the typical timeline for completing a design project?

Project timelines vary depending on the size and complexity of the project. A small residential project may take a few weeks, while a large commercial project could take several months. We will provide a detailed project timeline during the initial consultation.

5. Do you offer both residential and commercial interior design services?

Yes, we offer both residential and commercial interior design services. Our experienced team can tailor our services to meet the unique needs of each type of project.
